你换了个新发型吗
=== ANKI ===
Did you get a new hairstyle?

其实我已经换了新造型，只不过现在已经不是很明显了
=== ANKI ===
Actually, I’ve changed my style, but it hasn’t been noticed much.

最近我现在一直在掉头发
=== ANKI ===
I have been losing my hair continuously recently

因为它是由于雄激素太多
=== ANKI ===
because it’s due to too much testosterone.

你染过发吗？ 染过啊，现在我就是染过的。
=== ANKI ===
(Have you ever dyed your hair?) Yes, I have. I’ve dyed it before.

染发根还是发梢?
=== ANKI ===
Dyeing the roots or the ends

“去年的这个时候，我正在找工作，在现实世界中认识了一群新朋友。在那之前我只认识一些同学。我甚至和一个同性恋交了朋友。”
=== ANKI ===
“Last year around this time, I was on the job hunt and met a bunch of new people out in the real world. I only really knew folks from school before that. I even made friends with someone who’s gay.” (Informal - casual phrasing for a conversation with friends)

他告诉了我很多我以前不知道的事，同性恋们非常注重自己的外表，他会去美容机构打一种美容针。” translates to English as:
=== ANKI ===
“He told me many things I didn’t know before. Homosexuals pay great attention to their appearance, and he would go to a beauty institution to get a kind of beauty injection.”

我只是在试水，但那不是我的菜。这样有点麻烦，我更喜欢自然一点的状态。
=== ANKI ===
I was just testing the waters, but it wasn’t really my thing. It’s a bit troublesome like this, I prefer a more natural state.

一些老外会这么做，如果外国人不算的话，我只认识一个女孩有鼻环。
=== ANKI ===
“Some foreigners do this. If foreigners don’t count, I only know one girl who has a nose ring.”

之前我想这样干，但是在我周围没人打耳钉
=== ANKI ===
“I wanted to do it before, but nobody around me has pierced ears.”

有一个有趣的问题是，在古代，在两国边界，最开始他们是怎么互相理解对方的语言的”
=== ANKI ===
“One interesting question is, in ancient times, at the border of two countries, how did they initially understand each other’s language?”

你认为纹一个图案更有趣，如果是字的话就太直白了
=== ANKI ===
“Do you think that getting a tattoo with a design is more interesting? If it’s just words, it would be too straightforward.”

“这取决于职业，事实上，大部分黑社会都有纹身
=== ANKI ===
“It depends on the profession, in fact most gangsters have tattoos.”

我认为在中国有相同的趋势，最近我在练车，每天接送我去驾校的司机有很多纹身，他之前是黑社会，刚从监狱被释放。”
=== ANKI ===
“I think there is a similar trend in China. Recently, while I was practicing driving, the driver who drove me to the driving school every day had a lot of tattoos. He used to be a member of a gang and was just released from prison.”

在纹身文化中，一些图案有着特定的含义
=== ANKI ===
“In tattoo culture, some designs have specific meanings.

最近有个明星有一个扑克中的黑桃纹身，许多人批评她，因为这个纹身意味着崇拜黑人。
=== ANKI ===
“Lately, a celebrity has a spade tattoo from a deck of cards. Many people criticize her because this tattoo signifies admiration for black people.”

“不是理性崇拜，而是狂热崇拜。但是她不承认这一点。
=== ANKI ===
It’s not rational worship, but fanatic worship. But she doesn’t admit it.”

这取决于整容手术的效果吧，一些人不会说，但是她的脸就说明了一切
=== ANKI ===
“This depends on the effect of plastic surgery, some people won’t say it, but their face says it all.”

双眼皮手术是最多的，我之前认为只是女人做，认识那个同性恋后，知道男人做的也不少。
=== ANKI ===
“Double eyelid surgery is the most popular. I used to think only women did it, but after meeting that gay guy, I realized many men do it too.”

我们的课要结束了
=== ANKI ===
our lesson gonna end

去年我做了个手术，之后我的病好了一些，但是我的鼻子塌了，以前是直的，现在弯了。
=== ANKI ===
“Last year, I had surgery and since then my illness has improved somewhat, but my nose collapsed, it used to be straight, now it’s bent.”
