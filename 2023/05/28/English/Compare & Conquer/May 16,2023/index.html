当然，没问题!
=== ANKI ===
Sure, no problem at all!

每个农民都有很多亩地去耕作吗
=== ANKI ===
Does every farmer have many acres of land to cultivate?

你们的城市在平原吗
=== ANKI ===
Is your city on a plain?

在一些山区，农名们把地形改造成梯田，因为如果是一个坡的话，水土会很容易流失。
=== ANKI ===
In some mountainous areas, farmers transform the terrain into terraced fields because if it’s a slope, soil and water can be easily lost.

主要种一些容易成活的耐旱性作物
=== ANKI ===
Mainly plant crops that are easy to survive and drought-resistant.

煤产业最繁荣的时候是在2000年左右
=== ANKI ===
The peak of the coal industry was around the year 2000.

我们特别重视高考
=== ANKI ===
We place great importance on the college entrance examination.

那会是另一种庆祝，因为无论如何这是人生的一块里程碑
=== ANKI ===
That would be another kind of celebration because, in any case, it is a milestone in life.

这是中国的一种特殊的文化，不仅在现代，历来如此
=== ANKI ===
This is a unique culture in China, not only in modern times, but it has always been this way.

在古代中国，不管你出身如何都可以参加科举，如果你排名很高，那么你可以直接变成政府官员
=== ANKI ===
In ancient China, no matter your background, you could participate in the imperial examinations. If you ranked highly, then you could directly become a government official.

这种观念一直持续到了现在
=== ANKI ===
This ideology has persisted until now and is expected to continue in the future.

  • “ideology” is a heavier term and may imply a more structured or formalized set of beliefs than “concept”.
  • The verb “last” is typically used in simple past or present perfect tenses, such as “it lasted” or “it has lasted”.

这种观念根深蒂固
=== ANKI ===
This concept is deeply ingrained.

你可以申请国外的大学
=== ANKI ===
You can apply to universities abroad.

教育资源分配不均
=== ANKI ===
The distribution of educational resources is unequal.

  • Yes, you can use “uneven” in this context. The sentence would then be: “The distribution of educational resources is uneven.” In this context, “uneven” and “unequal” can be used interchangeably, both suggesting that resources are not equally or fairly distributed.

所以在多路径升学和单一路径之间存在一个矛盾
=== ANKI ===
So there is a contradiction between multiple paths to higher education and a single path.

每条路径的优势都可以悉数被富人们利用
=== ANKI ===
The advantages of every path can be fully exploited by the wealthy.

The advantages of every path can be fully utilized by the wealthy.

  • Yes, you can use “exploited” in this context. The sentence would then be: “The advantages of every path can be fully exploited by the wealthy.” However, please note that “exploited” often has a negative connotation, implying that the wealthy are taking unfair advantage of the system. If you want to keep a neutral tone, “utilized” might be a better choice.

这中间存在着大量的信息不对称
=== ANKI ===
There is a large amount of information asymmetry in between.

所以，在这种情况下，一条更加简单直接的道路可能是很重要的
=== ANKI ===
So, under these circumstances, a simpler and more direct path might be very important.

在中国很多地方，家庭条件不好的孩子们根本没有时间和条件参加各种课外活动
=== ANKI ===
In many parts of China, children from poor families simply do not have the time and conditions to participate in various extracurricular activities. can I say in some areas of China?

学生们有相同的课本、相同的考纲、相同的试卷，相同的录取标准
=== ANKI ===
Students have the same textbooks, the same syllabus, the same exams, and the same admission criteria.

申请的实质性指标只有你的分数
=== ANKI ===
The substantive criterion for applications is only your score.

就像我刚说的，这是一条很简单直接的路
=== ANKI ===
As I just mentioned, this is a very simple and direct path.

当然这在某种程度上也会束缚人们的创造力，让学生将大量的时间精力花在彼此的竞争上，但是没有办法，在一个发展中国家，教育往往不只是获得知识那么简单，更意味着社会资源的重新分配。
=== ANKI ===
Of course, to some extent, this also restricts people’s creativity, making students spend a lot of time and energy on competition with each other. But there is no other choice. In a developing country, education often means more than just acquiring knowledge, it also signifies the redistribution of social resources.
