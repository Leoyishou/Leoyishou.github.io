这个过程是，会有一个五个人的答辩委员会，他们会评估你的工作，投票决定你是否通过
=== ANKI ===
The process is that there will be a defense committee of five people. They will evaluate your work and vote on whether you pass or not.

答辩只是走个过场，外审才是实质性的一步
=== ANKI ===
The defense is just a formality, the external review is the substantive step.

所有人做完答辩后走出房间，各自的导师还有五个专家留在房间，导师向委员会介绍学生，然后专家们投票，产生决议。然后学生回到房间，
=== ANKI ===
everyone finishes their defense and leaves the room, each student’s advisor and five experts stay in the room. The advisor introduces the student to the committee, then the experts vote and reach a resolution. Afterwards, the students return to the room to hear the results.

那段时间我还沉浸在准备之中
=== ANKI ===
I was still immersed in my preparation.

I don’t have attention to others.
=== ANKI ===
I didn’t have the attention to spare for other people.

还出了点问题，不知道什么原因，我的ppt无法正常翻页
=== ANKI ===
I also had a bit of a problem. For some unknown reason, I couldn’t flip through my PowerPoint slides normally.

我们在等团队里的一个人，他回老家当伴郎去了
=== ANKI ===
We’re waiting for one person from our team who has gone back to his hometown to be a groomsman at a wedding.

我的同龄人很少有结婚的
=== ANKI ===
Very few of my peers are married.

我们只是在一个微信群里庆祝了一下
=== ANKI ===
We just celebrated a bit in a WeChat group.

给多少红包取决于你们的关系
=== ANKI ===
How much you put in the red envelope depends on your relationship with the person.

不同之处在于中国没有类似的party文化，但我认为心情是相同的
=== ANKI ===
The difference is that China does not have a similar party culture, but I believe the sentiment is the same.

当我们读硕士的时候，我们往往在课题组内活动，和班级同学没那么熟悉
=== ANKI ===
When we were pursuing our master’s degree, we usually interacted within our research group and were not as familiar with our classmates.

组内有一个导师来指导所有的硕士和博士
=== ANKI ===
In the group, there’s a mentor who guides all the master’s and doctoral students.

我之前还以为reception特指一些比较正式的场合
=== ANKI ===
I used to think that “reception” referred specifically to more formal occasions.

在我的中式思维中
=== ANKI ===
from my Chinese perspective.

有些人举办婚礼，会邀请我们全家参加
=== ANKI ===
When some people host weddings, they invite our whole family to attend.
